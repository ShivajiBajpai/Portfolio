About Shivaji

I'm a Digital Accessibility Consultant with over seven years of experience improving digital experiences across platforms. My passion for inclusive design stems from a belief that technology should empower everyone — regardless of ability.

I’ve contributed to accessibility audits and remediation efforts for major brands like Microsoft, LinkedIn, VMware, NetworkRail, and Prudential. My expertise spans WCAG, screen readers, ARIA, and a variety of automated tools.

Outside of work, I'm curious about everything from cricket and tech to politics and philosophy. I value simplicity, inclusivity, and continuous learning.
